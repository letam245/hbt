Heroes Bleed Too is a group of friends who works together at AMR in Redland. They are all EMT and paramedic who people usually viewed as heroes of the society. Everyday they drive and drive for every call to save lives. They have seen lots of bad and horrible situations, some cases are easy but somes they can never forget. Facing those stress and pressure everyday is part of their jobs and their lives but not many people know that behind their braveness, they are also humans who get terrify of what they have to handle or see at work, who are also get tired of life and also need to be saved. All the stereotype from the society for EMT/Paramedic along with all the dripping blood they encounter everyday at work have become a strong "CYNICAL INK" that ironically stamps in their lives. In the other hand, that "CYNICAL INK" also give them powerful inspiration for their love of music. To turn the negative experiences to better optimistic view, they decide to get together and form the band H.B.T or Heroes Bleed Too to pass their incredible stories back to the society. With all that intensity and dynamic emotion of their hero yet cynical background, their songs are wrote down and performed passionately whenever they step on stage.
